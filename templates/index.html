Global Landslide Susceptibility Map

Welcome to MapSnap, the ultimate tool for detecting landslides using satellite imagery and advanced deep learning technology. With our Segformer model, we analyze images pixel by pixel to quickly and accurately identify the areas affected. This means emergency responders and disaster management teams can act fast and potentially save lives.
MapSnap utilizes advanced deep learning technology and satellite imagery to quickly and accurately detect natural disasters. The core of this technology is the Segformer model, which analyzes images pixel by pixel to identify and segment affected areas. The model is trained on large datasets to recognize the unique features of various natural disasters, allowing it to effectively identify and segment affected areas in satellite imagery. When a disaster occurs, MapSnap's user-friendly interface allows emergency responders and disaster management teams to quickly input satellite imagery and receive detailed reports of affected areas. This provides essential information for prompt and effective response to disasters, potentially saving lives.
